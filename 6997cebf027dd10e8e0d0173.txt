The Subdomain Takeover exercise was originally demonstrated using helpjuice.com. However, due to recent updates, helpjuice.com has undergone a complete platform overhaul and now restricts registration to users with a recognized corporate email domain, blocking free email providers such as @gmail.com. As a result, this platform no longer serves as a suitable environment for practicing subdomain takeover in our current scenario.
That said, the underlying concepts remain fully applicable to other targets, and you are encouraged to explore them further. The exercise itself is still valid and worth reviewing for learning purposes.
